**1. Design Overview**

This document defines the complete visual design for CONNECT GREEN --- a
smart sustainable tourism platform. The design follows the style of the
Tulinovskyi Dribbble shot: \'Landing Page Concept for AI-Powered Wind
Energy Solution\' --- a dark, immersive green design with neon accents,
bold typography, and clean data-focused layouts.

**Design Style Summary**

  ---------------------- ------------------------------------------------
  **Design Property**    **Value / Description**

  **Style**              Dark modern web design with neon green glow
                         accents

  **Theme**              Eco-tech --- combines nature/green energy feel
                         with smart AI data aesthetics

  **Layout**             Full-width sections, spacious padding, split
                         hero (text left, visual right)

  **Mood**               Premium, trustworthy, forward-thinking,
                         environmentally powerful

  **Design Reference**   Dribbble shot #25290881 by Tulinovskyi --- AI
                         Wind Energy Landing Page
  ---------------------- ------------------------------------------------

**4. Page Layout --- Section by Section**

The website is built as a single-page scroll with clearly separated
sections. This matches the Tulinovskyi design\'s full-width,
scroll-based layout structure.

**4.1 Navbar (Top of Every Page)**

-   Background: dark green (#0B1A10) with a thin bright green bottom
    line

-   Left: CONNECT GREEN logo --- green leaf icon + white bold text

-   Center: Links --- Home, Trip Planner, Businesses, Nature Sites,
    Community

-   Right: Login (outlined) + Sign Up (solid neon green #22C55E) buttons

-   On scroll: navbar gets frosted blur background effect
    (backdrop-filter)

-   Mobile: hamburger icon opens a full-screen dark green overlay menu

**4.2 Hero Section**

> *This is the most important section --- modelled directly after the
> Tulinovskyi dark hero with split layout.*

  --------------------- -------------------------------------------------
  **Hero Element**      **Design Description**

  **Background**        Full screen height, dark green (#0B1A10), glowing
                        radial green gradient on right side

  **Layout**            Split: Left 55% = text content \| Right 45% =
                        large 3D/nature visual

  **Pill Tag**          Small capsule label: \'AI-Powered Sustainable
                        Tourism\' --- neon green text, dark green border

  **Main Headline**     \'Travel Green. Live Smart.\' --- White, Space
                        Grotesk Bold, 72px, letter-spacing tight

  **Subheading**        One line description of the platform ---
                        white/gray, Inter Regular, 18px

  **CTA Button 1**      \'Plan My Green Trip\' --- solid neon green
                        button, white text, rounded corners

  **CTA Button 2**      \'See How It Works\' --- transparent, green
                        border, white text

  **Hero Visual**       3D nature landscape / wind turbine illustration
                        OR animated Lottie green scene with glow effect

  **Stats Bar**         3 stats below buttons: \'500+ CO2 Tonnes Saved\',
                        \'200+ Green Businesses\', \'50 Sites Protected\'
  --------------------- -------------------------------------------------

**4.3 Features Section**

-   Background: light mint (#F0FDF4) --- switches from dark to light
    here

-   Section title centered: \'Everything You Need for a Green Trip\'

-   Layout: 3-column card grid (2 columns on tablet, 1 on mobile)

-   Each feature card: white background, green icon at top, title, short
    description

-   Card hover effect: thin neon green border glows on the card

-   6 feature cards: Trip Planner, Green Badge, Carbon Tracker, Site
    Monitor, Community Connector, Carbon Offset

**4.4 How It Works Section**

-   Background: white or very light green

-   Section title: \'How CONNECT GREEN Works\'

-   Layout: 3 numbered steps in a horizontal row

-   Each step: large circled number in neon green, icon, title, 2-line
    description

-   Step 1: Search your destination \| Step 2: Choose green options \|
    Step 3: Track your impact

-   Connecting dashed green line between the steps

**4.5 Nature Site Capacity Monitor (Showcase Section)**

-   Dark background section --- returns to #0B1A10 for contrast and
    visual impact

-   Left side: text --- explain the live capacity monitoring feature

-   Right side: mock UI widget showing a nature site with
    Green/Yellow/Red traffic light badge

-   Animated pulsing green dot on the \'Green - Safe to Visit\' state

-   This section showcases the most unique feature of the platform
    visually

**4.6 Carbon Footprint Tracker (Data Section)**

-   Light background section

-   Centered stat showing \'Total CO2 Saved by CONNECT GREEN Users: 500+
    Tonnes\'

-   Large animated circular progress ring in neon green showing user
    carbon score

-   Two comparison columns: \'Your Trip Carbon\' vs \'Average Tourist
    Trip Carbon\'

-   Matches the Tulinovskyi AI data panel aesthetic --- glowing numbers
    on clean background

**4.7 Green Badge Business Section**

-   Show 3--4 sample verified business cards in a horizontal row

-   Each card: business image, name, category, and their Green Badge
    (Bronze/Silver/Gold/Platinum)

-   Badge is displayed as a colored icon with glow matching badge tier
    color

-   CTA button: \'Browse All Green Businesses\'

**4.8 Testimonials Section**

-   3 user review cards on a light background

-   Each card: quote text, user avatar circle, name, and green star
    rating

-   Cards have slight shadow and a thin green top border

**4.9 Call to Action (Final Section)**

-   Full-width dark green background section (#0B1A10)

-   Centered large text: \'Start Your Green Journey Today\'

-   Large neon green CTA button: \'Create Free Account\'

-   Subtle green glowing background effect (radial gradient) centered
    behind the text

**4.10 Footer**

-   Dark background (#0B1A10), 4 columns: Logo+tagline, Navigation,
    Resources, Social links

-   Thin neon green top border line separating footer from content

-   Copyright line at very bottom in small gray text

**7. Icons & Visual Elements**

**7.1 Icons**

-   Icon library: Lucide Icons or Heroicons (both free and open source)

-   Icon size in feature cards: 32x32px in a 56x56px rounded square
    container

-   Icon container color: dark green (#0D2818) on light sections,
    slightly lighter on dark sections

-   Icon color: neon green (#22C55E) --- creates the glowing energy-tech
    look from the reference design

**7.2 Hero Visual**

-   Option A (Recommended): 3D rendered green landscape with a glowing
    wind turbine --- matches reference design

-   Option B: Lottie animated nature scene (trees, wind, sun) ---
    lighter file size

-   Option C: High quality free image from Unsplash --- search \'green
    nature aerial sustainable\'

-   The visual should have a dark-to-transparent fade on its left edge
    so it blends into the dark background

**7.3 Background Effects**

-   Hero section: radial gradient glow --- CSS: radial-gradient(ellipse
    at 80% 50%, rgba(34,197,94,0.15) 0%, transparent 60%)

-   CTA section: same radial glow centered --- creates the \'energy
    core\' look from Tulinovskyi\'s design

-   Cards on dark bg: subtle inner glow border on hover --- box-shadow:
    0 0 0 1px #22C55E

**8. Responsive Design (Mobile-Friendly)**

The design must work on all screen sizes --- desktop, tablet, and mobile
phone.

  --------------- ----------------- --------------------------------------
  **Screen**      **Width**         **Design Adjustments**

  **Desktop**     1280px and above  Full layout --- split hero, 3-column
                                    features, side-by-side sections

  **Tablet**      768px -- 1279px   2-column features, hero stack (visual
                                    below text), smaller fonts

  **Mobile**      Below 768px       Single column, hamburger menu, hero
                                    visual hidden or small, full-width
                                    buttons

**10. Wireframe Layout Descriptions**

Below are simple text wireframe descriptions for each main page to guide
front-end development.

**10.1 Landing Page (Home)**

> *Top to bottom scroll: Navbar → Hero (dark) → Features (light) → How
> It Works (white) → Site Monitor Showcase (dark) → Carbon Data (light)
> → Businesses Strip → Testimonials → CTA (dark) → Footer (dark)*

**10.2 Trip Planner Page**

> *Left panel (35%): Search inputs (from, to, dates, filters). Right
> area (65%): Full interactive Google Map. Bottom strip: Suggested green
> hotels, transport, restaurants. Carbon meter widget fixed at bottom
> right corner.*

**10.3 Green Business Directory**

> *Top: Filter bar (category, location, badge tier). Below: Card grid
> --- 3 per row on desktop, each card has image, business name, badge
> icon, short description, and \'View\' button.*

**10.4 Nature Sites Page**

> *Top: Site search + map toggle button. Below: List of site cards, each
> showing: Site name, location, category, live traffic light capacity
> badge (Green/Yellow/Red), and \'View Details\' button.*

**10.5 Tourist Dashboard**

> *Header: Welcome message + green points score. Top row: 3 stat cards
> --- Trips Planned, Total CO2 Saved, Green Points. Middle: Carbon
> progress ring (big, animated). Bottom: Trip history table with green
> badge tags.*

**10.6 Login / Register Page**

> *Centered card on dark green page background. Card: Logo at top, form
> fields (email, password), role selector buttons (Tourist / Business /
> Site Manager), social login buttons, submit button in neon green.*
