**1. Executive Summary**

CONNECT GREEN is a smart, web-based platform designed to unify the
fragmented landscape of sustainable tourism. The platform acts as a
central digital ecosystem that connects eco-conscious tourists, verified
green businesses, and protected natural sites --- enabling fully green
travel experiences from planning to completion.

The platform addresses a critical gap: while green hotels,
eco-transport, organic restaurants, and natural conservation zones exist
independently, there is no single trusted platform that connects them
all. CONNECT GREEN solves this by providing an integrated, AI-powered,
data-driven web platform where tourists can plan, track, and complete
100% green trips while actively contributing to environmental
protection.

**2. Project Overview**

**2.1 Project Title**

CONNECT GREEN: A Smart Web Platform for Sustainable Tourism

**2.2 Project Domain**

Green Technologies for a Sustainable Future / Sustainable Tourism /
Environmental Technology

**2.3 Problem Statement**

Tourism is one of the world\'s largest industries, yet it contributes
significantly to carbon emissions, ecosystem destruction, and resource
overconsumption. Key problems include:

-   Tourists have no single trusted platform to plan a fully green trip
    end-to-end

-   Greenwashing is rampant --- businesses falsely claim eco-friendly
    status with no verification

-   Natural sites suffer from over-tourism with no real-time capacity
    management

-   Green businesses (hotels, restaurants, transport) lack visibility
    and connection to eco-conscious tourists

-   Tourists are unaware of the carbon footprint generated by their
    travel choices

-   There is no platform that rewards sustainable behavior or connects
    tourists to local green communities

**2.4 Proposed Solution**

CONNECT GREEN is a smart web platform that:

1.  Connects tourists, green businesses, and nature sites in one unified
    ecosystem

2.  Verifies and certifies eco-friendly businesses through a Green Badge
    system

3.  Provides real-time carbon footprint tracking for every trip

4.  Monitors natural site capacity using IoT sensors to prevent
    over-tourism

5.  Recommends low-carbon routes and green alternatives using AI

6.  Connects tourists to local organic farms, eco-guides, and green
    artisans

**2.5 Project Goals**

-   Reduce the carbon footprint of tourism by enabling smarter, greener
    travel choices

-   Create a verified, trustworthy ecosystem of green-certified
    businesses

-   Protect natural ecosystems from over-tourism through real-time
    monitoring

-   Educate and empower tourists to make environmentally responsible
    decisions

-   Support local green economies by connecting them directly with
    eco-conscious tourists

**4. Functional Requirements**

**4.1 User Registration & Profile Management**

-   Users can register as Tourist, Business Owner, or Nature Site
    Manager

-   Profile setup includes preferences: travel style, carbon goals,
    interests

-   OAuth 2.0 login support (Google, Facebook) alongside email/password

-   Tourists can view their green travel history, carbon savings, and
    badges earned

**4.2 Green Trip Planner**

-   Interactive map-based trip planner with start/end destination input

-   AI engine suggests eco-friendly itineraries (green hotels,
    transport, food)

-   Filter options: carbon score, proximity, green certification level,
    cost

-   Compare itineraries side-by-side based on CO2 impact vs. traditional
    trips

-   Export trip plan as PDF or share via link

**4.3 Green Verification & Badge System**

-   Businesses submit an application form with evidence of green
    practices

-   Platform admin team reviews against a defined Green Criteria
    Checklist

-   Verified businesses receive tiered badges: Bronze, Silver, Gold,
    Platinum Green

-   Badge criteria includes: renewable energy use, waste reduction,
    water efficiency, local sourcing

-   Badges are publicly visible on business profiles and searchable on
    the platform

-   Annual re-verification required to maintain badge status

**4.4 Live Carbon Footprint Tracker**

-   Real-time CO2 calculation as tourists build their itinerary

-   Carbon metrics sourced from transport mode, distance, accommodation
    type, and food choices

-   Dashboard displays: Your Trip Carbon vs. Average Tourist Carbon

-   Carbon offset portal: tourists can fund verified green projects
    (tree planting, solar, wind)

-   Gamification: earn green points and levels for low-carbon travel
    choices

**4.5 Nature Site Capacity Monitor**

-   Integration with IoT sensors at registered nature sites (parks,
    beaches, forests)

-   Live visitor count displayed as a traffic-light system: Green /
    Yellow / Red

-   When a site reaches capacity, platform automatically suggests
    alternative nearby sites

-   Site managers receive alerts and can set capacity limits via their
    dashboard

-   Historical capacity data and trends available for environmental
    impact reporting

**4.6 Local Green Community Connector**

-   Directory of verified local eco-guides, organic farms, artisan
    markets, and green experiences

-   Tourists can book directly through the platform with integrated
    payment

-   Community members can create profiles, list services, and manage
    bookings

-   Rating and review system for all community listings

-   Language support for local community members in their native
    language

**4.7 Sustainability Analytics Dashboard**

-   Available to government bodies, tourism boards, and platform admins

-   Aggregated metrics: total CO2 saved, number of green trips planned,
    green business growth

-   Heatmaps showing tourist distribution across natural sites

-   Downloadable sustainability reports in PDF/Excel formats

-   AI-generated recommendations for improving regional sustainable
    tourism policies

**4.8 Notification & Alert System**

-   Email and in-app notifications for trip reminders, badge renewals,
    site capacity alerts

-   Push notifications for tourists when a saved site reaches Yellow or
    Red capacity status

-   Business owners notified of badge expiry and renewal deadlines

**11. Constraints & Assumptions**

**11.1 Constraints**

-   Platform must be accessible on web browsers (Chrome, Firefox,
    Safari, Edge) and mobile browsers

-   IoT sensor integration is dependent on nature site management
    cooperation

-   Carbon footprint calculations are based on published emission factor
    databases (IPCC, DEFRA)

-   Green badge verification process requires human admin review ---
    cannot be fully automated

-   Payment processing subject to applicable financial regulations in
    operating regions

**11.2 Assumptions**

-   Nature site managers are willing to install or provide access to
    visitor counting sensors

-   Green businesses understand the value of certification and will
    provide accurate documentation

-   Target users have access to smartphones or computers with internet
    connectivity

-   Carbon offset projects partnered with the platform are verified by
    recognized third-party standards (Gold Standard, VCS)

-   The platform will launch in English with multi-language support
    added in Phase 2

**12. Glossary**

  --------------------- -------------------------------------------------
  **Term**              **Definition**

  **Carbon Footprint**  The total greenhouse gas emissions caused
                        directly or indirectly by an individual or
                        activity

  **Green Badge**       A verified certification awarded to businesses
                        meeting CONNECT GREEN eco-criteria

  **Carbon Offset**     A reduction in emissions made to compensate for
                        emissions produced elsewhere

  **IoT Sensor**        Internet of Things device used to monitor
                        real-time environmental or occupancy data

  **Greenwashing**      Misleading claims about the environmental
                        practices of a business or product

  **Eco-System**        In platform context: an interconnected network of
                        tourists, businesses, and nature sites

  **GDPR**              General Data Protection Regulation --- EU data
                        privacy law applicable to user data

  **NPS**               Net Promoter Score --- a measure of user
                        satisfaction and loyalty
